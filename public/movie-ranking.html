The Best Movies According to Solomon

My Top Four Movies of All Time

Mission: Impossible – The Final Reckoning

Tom Cruise returns for jaw-dropping stunts and high-stakes thrills in this blockbuster action-spy finale.

Mickey 17

Bong Joon Ho’s latest sci-fi-black comedy starring Robert Pattinson as a “disposable” worker on a space colony.

Past Lives

A beautifully understated drama by Celine Song. It explores themes of love, fate, and cultural identity.

Dune: Part Two

Denis Villeneuve's epic sci-fi continuation of Paul Atreides' journey on Arrakis. A visual spectacle with deep world-building-critics and fans alike have praised it as a worthy follow-up to the first film
